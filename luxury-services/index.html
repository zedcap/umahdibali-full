SERVICES

HOLIDAY BLISS

RELAX, UNWIND AND INDULGE

At umah di bali, we want your holiday to be as carefree and relaxing as possible.

Our daily villa attendant will take care of your cleaning needs, and our villa manager is available to assist you with any needs you may have during your stay. Upon request, a chef will prepare meals at umah di bali, or simply finger food to accompany your pre-dinner drinks.

So sit back, relax and let us take care of you from the moment you arrive at the airport. We look forward to making your time at umah di bali a truly memorable experience.

SPOIL YOURSELF

FOOD & DRINKS

A great holiday experience often goes with exciting options and settings for meals and cocktails.

Berawa area offers plenty of great food options, from healthy cafes to trendy cuisine from all around the world. And for those who enjoy a refreshing drink, there are many bars and beach clubs that serve creative cocktails, perfect for a sunset at the nearby beach.

To make your stay even more special, our villa manager can even book a chef for a special occasion.

RELAX & REJUVENATE

MASSAGE

Enjoy a relaxing Balinese massage without leaving the comfort and privacy of umah di bali.

Choose from a traditional Bali full body massage to head or foot massage, or a spa treatment including facials, manicure and pedicure.

The perfect way to unwind after a beach day.

AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

CONNECTIVITY

Our bedrooms are equipped with top-notch amenities including flat screen TVs, satellite receiver, and Apple TV to make sure you never miss your favorite shows while relaxing after a day in the sun.

We also provide high-speed Wi-Fi access from all rooms, including the terraces, so you can stay connected with your loved ones or work from the comfort of your villa.

GETTING AROUND

TRANSPORTATION

Simply ask our villa manager to organise an airport transfer to arrive worry-free in Bali.

If you want to explore the island, our villa manager will be delighted to assist you in booking motorbikes or cars, whether it’s for a day trip or for your entire stay.

JUST ASK US

ANYTHING ELSE?

If you need a baby sitter, a tour guide, to book a tee time at Bali National Golf Club, or just some tips on how to haggle at street markets, our villa manager is always available to help.

Don’t hesitate to ask, we are more than happy to make your stay in Bali as comfortable and enjoyable as possible.
