UMAH DI BALI

LAVISHNESS AT THE HEART OF CANGGU’S MOST VIBRANT DISTRICT

An inspiring modern sanctuary designed with a Mediterranean signature touch, using Palimanan white stones and framed with arched windows and doors.

Designed by one of the top Architecture and Design firms from Bali, the townhouse features an integrated open-plan living area including living, dining and kitchen. The living space opens on a private lounge area and pool through elegant connecting doors, and on an open terrace on the opposite side.

A stylish light well connecting the two floors brightens the entire space and brings a touch of vegetation in the living room. The sleeping area is nestled on the first floor, featuring 2 ensuite bedrooms, perfect for family living or sharing amongst friends.

UMAH DI BALI

LUXURY TOWNHOUSE

Umah di Bali is located just 800 meters from Berawa beach, and within walking distance of Canggu’s incredible bars and restaurants scene. Whether you’re a family or a group of friends, this stunning two-story townhouse offers the perfect retreat for those seeking the finest experiences.

The two-story townhouse features two luxurious bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, and provides ample space to relax or entertain in a beautifully blended indoor-outdoor setting.

TWO SEPARATE SPACES WHERE TO SIT BACK AND ENJOY

LOUNGE & TERRACE

The living space opens up to a beautiful open-air lounge area with a refreshing plunge pool, the perfect place to unwind after a day of exploring the bustling surroundings.

The other side of the living area opens up to an open terrace, with large sliding doors that let in the cool breeze and natural light.

Unwind in a serene environment, ideal for unwinding or hosting gatherings with friends and loved ones.

HIGHEST QUALITY FURNITURE AND APPLIANCES

LIVING & DINING

Umah di Bali features an integrated open-plan living area that seamlessly blends living, dining, and kitchen spaces, complete with a sleek island/bar that’s perfect for entertaining.

Tastefully furnished with the highest quality appliances, like wine fridge or induction cooking, this is a space where style and functionality meet in perfect harmony.

A stylish light well connecting the two floors brightens the entire space, while bringing a touch of lush vegetation into the living room.

RELAX AND ENJOY

BEDROOMS

Discover the ultimate in comfort and style with our two air-conditioned bedrooms, featuring ample space and a dressing room for your convenience. Each bedroom comes with an ensuite bathroom, complete with walk-in showers and adorned with natural materials and high-end finishes.
