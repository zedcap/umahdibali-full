LOCATION

BALI’S MOST VIBRANT NEIGHBORHOOD

LAVISHNESS AT THE HEART OF BERAWA

Only 800 metres away from the beach, umah di bali is at the heart of Bali’s most vibrant district, Berawa, considered the destination of choice for those who like the finest of experiences.

One glimpse at the vibrant bars and restaurant scene, world-class waves, and panoramic view of the Indian Ocean and it’s clear just why this bustling neighbourhood is quickly becoming everyone’s favorite, from long term residents to seasoned expatriates.

YOUR HOME AT THE HEART OF BERAWA

UMAH DI BALI

Location, location, location! Umah di bali is ideally situated at the center of Berawa on a quiet dead-end street, just a short 800-meter walk away from the beach. But don’t let the peaceful surroundings fool you. We’re also in the heart of a bustling dining and entertainment scene that’s second to none.

Be sure to check out our in-house guest directory, which is available both at umah di bali and online, for our latest recommendations on the best places to eat, drink, and have fun in the area.

ENJOY WHAT BALI DOES BEST

WINE & DINE

Berawa is a neighborhood that’s really come into its own over the years, thanks in no small part to its world-class dining and entertainment scene.

Whether you’re in the mood for classic French, Italian, Asian, or a romantic beachside dinner, you’re sure to find what you’re looking for here. Some of our favorites include Riviera, Ulekan, Miss Fish, and One Eyed Jack.

Berawa is also home to some of the most amazing beach clubs in Bali. You won’t want to miss out on the incredible experiences to be had at Café del Mar, Morabito, Finn’s Beach Club, and the upcoming Savaya Canggu, all just a short walk away.

CATCH THE WAVES

BEACH TIME

Calling all surfers, whether you’re a pro or just starting out, Canggu’s beaches offer some of the best waves around. At Old Man’s, you’ll find plenty of surf schools to choose from, as well as some more challenging breaks for the more experienced surfers out there.

But if you’re looking for a more laid-back beach experience, we’ve got you covered too. Head on over to Caravan or the Naked Coconut on Berawa beach and settle into a comfy day bed next to one of the world-famous beach clubs. There’s no better way to relax and soak up the sun.

No matter what kind of beach day you’re in the mood for, umah di bali has got you covered.

DISCOVER BALI

DAY TRIPS

Looking to explore all the amazing things Bali has to offer? We’ve got you covered.

Let us organize a car and driver for you to go watch the sunset at Tanah Lot, or head south to Bukit to surf at some of Bali’s best beach breaks.

If you’re up for a day trip, why not head north to Ubud? Discover the stunning rice terraces at Tegalalang, indulge in some of Ubud’s acclaimed day spas, and visit some of the many temples along the way.
